Why Join a Club at University?

Pursue a passion

Whether it's creativity, community service, or sports, there is a club for everyone at Ambo University. Get involved in what you love and grow your skills.

Build Connections

Meet new people, make friends, and expand your network by joining student communities across campus.

Develop Skills

Gain leadership, teamwork, and practical experience that will help you in your academic and professional journey.

Improve Career Oportunities

Gaining experience and achievementsthat make your CVstronger and prepare you for future jobs..

Increase Confidence

Becoming more comfortable expressing your ideas, speaking in front of others, and taking responsibility.

Gain Real Life Experience

Learning by doing practical tasks like organizing events, managing projects,and working in teams.

Leadership Oportunities

Taking responsibility for guiding a group, organizing activities, and making decisions that help the club suceed.

problem Solving Skills

Developing the ability to analyze challenges and find effective solutions through teamwork and critical thinking.

Tsega Bona

PEACE FORUM PRESIDENT

“Joining the Peace Forum is a great opportunity for students who want to grow in leadership, communication, and teamwork. Through serving in the club, members gain experience in organizing events, speaking confidently, resolving conflicts peacefully, and working with different groups of students. Beyond activities, the club helps students build strong friendships, responsibility, and a sense of purpose while creating a positive campus environment.”

Obsa Birhanu

Serving in the club also helps students become more confident leaders and better communicators for their future careers.”

Amanuel Abera
